The Effects of Entrepreneurial Management on the Creation and Growth of Startups

Mohammad Hossein Shamsini Ghiasvand :Mohammad Hossein Shamsini Ghiasvand

Entrepreneurial management, as an innovative and strategic process, plays a crucial role in the establishment and development of startups. This article examines the key impacts of entrepreneurial management on the creation and growth of new ventures. Initially, the processes of opportunity identification and guiding innovative ideas through effective management lead to value creation and the emergence of new markets. Furthermore, the managerial and leadership skills of entrepreneurs are essential for motivating teams, attracting investors, and retaining human resources. Research indicates that startups operating under strong entrepreneurial management are generally better equipped to confront market challenges and achieve sustainable success. Ultimately, the article concludes that investing in entrepreneurial management skills not only fosters the creation of new businesses but also ensures their long-term growth and development. Therefore, attention to this dimension is vital in both economic and educational planning. : Entrepreneurial management, as an innovative and strategic process, plays a crucial role in the establishment and development of startups.
